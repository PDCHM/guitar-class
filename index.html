Guitar Class — Peter

A private learning platform for guitar students. Access your lesson resources, submit practice videos, and receive personalised feedback — all in one place.

What's inside

Lesson Resources
Access songs, chord sheets, audio references and video guides assigned to your class level.
Submit Practice Videos
Record and upload your practice videos directly from your phone or computer for review.
Personalised Feedback
Receive written feedback from your teacher on every submission to track your progress.
Class Community
See what your classmates are working on and stay motivated together as a group.

How it works

1

2

3
Submit & get feedback
Upload your practice video and receive personalised feedback from your teacher.
